Omelette

THE EASIEST EGG RECIPE THERE IS THAT ISN'T JUST BOILING AN EGG!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp butter

Steps

  1. Season the beaten eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan over a medium-low heat until butter has melted.
  3. Pour eggs into pan and cook for 20 secs.
  4. Tilt the pan around allowing the pan to fill with runny egg and repeat this two more times.
  5. Add whatever you have in the fridge to the omelette.
