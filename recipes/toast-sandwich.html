Toast Sandwich

THE FOOD OF THE HIGHLY DERANGED

Ingredients

  • 3 Slices of Bread
  • Butter if you're wealthy

Steps

  1. Toast one of the slices of bread.
  2. Butter the toast if you're part of the Bourgeoisie.
  3. Chuck the toasted slice between the untoasted slices.
  4. Question how it came to this.
